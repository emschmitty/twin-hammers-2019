At the heart of the LaCroix Hydration Club—and our spirit challenge— is the creation and consumption of the Rainbow of Hydration! Spirit Winners begin their journey to total hydration by going on a treasure hunt slash obstacle course. Their goal? To find their very own Spirit Flavor (some people have spirit animals; our people have Spirit Flavors). Our Spirit Winners embark on this journey by turning over a disc—this will unlock their Flavor Team.

The Spirit Winners will search for the LaCroix Hydration Club members that matches their given flavor—this club member will be their Flavor Spirit Guide. The Flavor Spirit Guide will then gift the Spirit Winner with a bottle of flavor—the first step in creating their Spirit Flavor.

Now comes the obstacle course! Spirit Winners must first knock down a wall of cans with a disc. When successful, they'll discover the cup in which they will create their Spirit Flavor.

Next, Spirit Winners will proceed to locate a can of Pure LaCroix, searching for the cherished item in a bucket of empty cans. To make things even more interesting, they must retrieve the can using only their mouths.

The Spirit Winners are now almost ready to create their very own spirit flavors!

But first... They will need to pass a whipped cream flip contest. (Naturally.)

Once successful, the Spirit Winners will proceed to the finish line where—at last—they will Create and Hydrate with their very own LACROIX SPIRIT FLAVOR.

Winners will not only be gifted with a goodie bag of hydration-related items, they'll also receive the ultimate prize—TOTAL HYDRATION!
