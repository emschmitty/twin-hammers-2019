About Twin Hammers

About Us


We’re a group of DiscNW Fall Hat rag-tag orphans that came together to form a forever family for Frisbee tournaments in and around the PNW.

The core team started back in 2015, attending Hanford Howl as "Marvel's Misfits." We proceeded to attend Hanford and Sunbreak nearly every year since. Last year at Sunbreak, we decided to adopt the "Twin Hammers" moniker and we haven't looked back since!

MARVEL'S MISFITS - Hanford Howl 2015

This was the first tournament the crew attended. We dressed up as "rejected" superheroes such as Captain Canada, Poor Batman, Captain Hindsight, The Flasher, and Quail Man. Little did we know that this tournament was just the beginning...
AVATAR: THE LAST AIRBENDER - Sunbreak 2016

The team's first Sunbreak, we decided to go as the amazing children's cartoon, Avatar: The Last Airbender. The weekend featured characters such as Korra, Aang, Oppa, and the Cabbage Guy. Our favorite in-game points were cabbage points, which replaced the disc with a head of lettuce.
WOODY'S ROUNDUP - Hanford Howl 2016

We decided to follow up Hanford as everyone's favorite Pixar film, Toy Story. From the little green aliens to Mr. Potato Head to Mrs. Nesbit (aka Buzz drunk on tea) to Bo Peep and more, we made sure the whole toy gang was thoroughly represented (Woody was there, too, but didn't make it into this photo...). The costume MVP was Slink, which was played by 2 players; ducttape operated as Slink's "slinky."
DR. HORRIBLE'S SING ALONG BLOG - Hanford Howl 2017

Beware of the "Ice" Beam! Highlights from this tournament included the "Parade of Nations," during which our Captain Hammer got with hit an "Ice Ray" and had to drink an ice-related beverage whilst marching spirit-ably. As a collection of eclectic villains, we took Hanford by force and brought the spirit.
TWIN HAMMERS YACHT CLUB - Sunbreak 2018

This tournament brought on our first use of the "Twin Hammers" name. We posed as members of the "most inclusive yacht club between the Montlake Cut and the Sixty Acres Sea". Hammers were a staple as we soaked up the sun and brought the fun. Check out our bid video from last year here.
TWIN HAMMERS IKEA - Hanford Howl 2018

We followed up our nautical adventure by paying homage to the Swedish megastore, IKEA. It all started when one of our teammates had a burning desire to dress up as a chair. Other teammates attended as various items related to the IKEA shopping experience: the ubiquitous blue IKEA shopping bag, store employees, customers, cafeteria food (like IKEA’s famous Swedish meatballs), and more. The highlight of this theme was the spirit game, which had Spirit Winners live out a day in an IKEA store, making their way through an obstacle course while collecting materials to construct their very own IKEA furniture piece—with only an ambiguous instruction manual to guide them. (Yes, one of our teammate's dressed as an instruction manual).
TWIN HAMMERS...AND THAT PAMPLEMOUSSE - Sunbreak 2019

Our goal this year at Sunbreak (should our bid be accepted) is to ensure that all our opponents stay hydrated and refreshe—all thanks to the cool, bubbly goodness of LaCroix. What flavor are YOU?
