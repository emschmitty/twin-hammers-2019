LaCroix, With a Twist

LaCroix is fancy, but what if you want to really jazz it up. Just imagine struting down the sidelines with your own fabulous LaCroix mocktail in hand.

Fun in the Sun

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Can Lemon LaCroix
  • 1/2 Can Coconut LaCroix
  • 1 oz Pineapple Juice

Directions

Mix lemon and coconut LaCroix and add pineapple juice. Enjoy!

Mangojito

Ingredients

  • 4 mint leaves
  • 2 lime wedges
  • 5 oz Mango LaCroix

Directions

Muddle mint leaves and 1 lime wedge in bottom of glass. Atop with mango LaCroix. Add crushed ice and garnish with remaining lime wedge.

Mimosa Mocktails

Ingredients

  • 2 oz fresh-squeezed orange juice

  • 4 oz. Orange LaCroix

Directions

Muddle mint leaves and 1 lime wedge in bottom of glass. Atop with mango LaCroix. Add crushed ice and garnish with remaining lime wedge.

Strawberry Basil Perfection

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Basil Leaves
  • 1 tsp. balsamic vinegar
  • 1 cup Berry LaCroix

Directions

Muddle 3 medium-size strawberries, basil leaves and balsamic vinegar in a mixing glass. Add Berry LaCroix Sparkling Water. Top with fresh cracked black pepper for a final flourish and serve.
