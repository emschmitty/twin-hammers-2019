Welcome to Twin Hammers... And That Pamplemousse

#TakeYourHydrationSeriously


La Croix doesn’t need more evangelists, but we’re going to #preach anyway. After all, with the hot days of summer upon us, we want to proclaim to the world that “IT’S TIME TO TAKE YOUR HYDRATION SERIOUSLY!” (#TakeYourHydrationSeriously).

Twin Hammers... And That Pamplemousse will provide teammates, opponents, and passersby some much needed refreshments over at 60 Acres, whether they are all about that #PeachPearDontCare life, want to live their own Tangerine dream, are Berry jazzed for Cran-Raz, want a wellness shot of Apricot, or are looking to discover their one true Passion(fruit). We’re here to help them (Man)go (Coco)nuts and fuel their lives with Pure bliss.

Our half-time and time-out Bubble Bar will introduce interested field attendees to some of the finest artisanal mocktails — aka, LaCroixtails (pronounced La-Cwock-Tails) — around, and the post-game Spirit Challenge will feature Spirit Winners partaking in a La Croix-based treasure hunt that ends in a boat race to see who can finish all their La Croix first. We’ll be crowning a La Croix Roi, if you will. (Fun fact: “Roi” is French for king.)

In the spirit of encouraging our fellow tourney goers to take their hydration seriously (#TakeYourHydrationSeriously), we’ll be generous with our La Croix stash (though a little friendly bartering may be in order)—after all, dehydration is the enemy.

All the while, we’ll seek to answer the age-old question, WHAT FLAVOR OF LACROIX ARE YOU?
