Why Do We Want to Attend Sunbreak 2019 as a LaCroix-Themed Team?

LaCroix doesn’t need more evangelists, but we’re going to #preach anyway. After all, with the hot days of summer upon us, we want to proclaim to the world that “IT’S TIME TO TAKE YOUR HYDRATION SERIOUSLY!”

(#TakeYourHydrationSeriously)

Twin Hammers...And That Pamplemousse are looking to provide teammates, opponents, and passersby over at 60 Acres some much needed refreshments, whether they are all about that #PeachPearDontCare life, want to live their own Tangerine dream, are Berry jazzed for Cran-Raz, want a wellness shot of Apricot, or are looking to discover their one true Passion(fruit). We’re here to help them (Man)go (Coco)nuts and fuel their lives with Pure bliss.

Our half-time and time-out Bubble Bar will introduce interested field attendees to some of the finest artisanal mocktails—aka, LaCroixtails (pronounced "La-Cwock-Tails")—around. Simultaneously, players can participate in a competitive LaCroixtail-themed fashion show where contestants create new mix-and-match flavors (in the words of Big Dipper, it's all about that "mélange in your mouth") by dressing up a teammate with flavor-inspired gear. Contestants are scored on creativity, flare, and how easy it is to guess the brand new flavor combo. Winners will receive a delightful surprise that'll keep them inspired to take their hydration seriously.

The fun doesn't stop there! We'll invite the Spirit Winners from each game to participate in our post-game Spirit Challenge during which they'll be presented with an opportunity to drink the rainbow. You can read all about it on our Spirit Game page.

In the spirit of encouraging our fellow tourney goers to take their hydration seriously (#TakeYourHydrationSeriously), we’ll be generous with our La Croix stash (though a little friendly bartering may be in order)—after all, dehydration is the enemy.

All the while, we’ll seek to answer the age-old question, WHAT FLAVOR OF LACROIX ARE YOU?
